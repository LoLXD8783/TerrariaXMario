[c/d91616:Mario] and [c/1b9504:Luigi]: Escapade
The definitive Terraria x Mario experience


[c/ff0000:Prelude]
This mod is in its very early stages. Treat this version as a sort-of "development alpha build". Not all content and mechanics have been added, and existing ones have not been heavily balanced or brute-tested. It is recommended that you create a new world with this mod on, and not play with other major content mods or with other people. This mod has little to none multiplayer / mod compatability.

Rest assured that everything is being worked on including the aforementioned shortcomings.

This build mainly serves to establish important systems, such as the loadout, powerups, and transformations. In the future, expect lots of Mario representation, including enemies, Powerups, items, and even entire subworlds to explore!
